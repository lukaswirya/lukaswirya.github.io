Irish Cream Coffee

~TheSavoury~

Ingredients

  • Whipping Cream
  • Brown Sugar
  • Irish Whiskey
  • Coffee

An easy recipe for Irish cream coffee, an alcoholic drink prepared with coffee and whisky, added with a soft cream.

Lightly whip the cream - it should not be stiff.

Lightly heat the whiskey, but do not let it boil.

Stir in the brown sugar, stirring until dissolved, and pour into heat-resistant glasses.

Fill the glasses just over half-full with hot coffee.

Slowly add the lightly whipped cream by pouring it over the back of a spoon.

Decorate the glasses with a sugared rim if you wish.

To do this, whisk 1 egg white and put it on a shallow plate.

Put a little sugar on another plate.

Dip the rim of each glass first in the egg white and then in the sugar.
