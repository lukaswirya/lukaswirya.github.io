Honey And Soy Glazed Sesame Tofu

~TheSavoury~

Ingredients

  • Honey
  • Lime Juice
  • Tofu
  • Sesame
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Bamboo Steakers

Looking for a tasty vegetarian appetizer? Try these sesame crusted tofu skewers, glazed in honey and soy. An easy recipe perfect if you like Japanese flavours.

Whisk the first 4 ingredients together. Marinate the tofu in the mixture for at least 2 hours. Preheat a barbecue. Thread the tofu onto prepared pre-soaked skewers. Cook over a medium heat for 1 to 2 minutes each side, basting with any remaining glaze. Transfer to a serving dish. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and serve, garnished with lime curls
